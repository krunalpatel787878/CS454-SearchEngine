Atif Aslam
January 15 at 8:55pm ·

2015 was a hectic and busy year for myself and the team. MASHALLAH our live concerts and world tour touched 52 cities across 21 countries within 12 months!! (From Jan to June in South Africa, India, Kenya, UAE, Mauritius, Turkey & Aug to Dec in USA, Germany, Hungary, Kuwait, Pakistan, UK, Holland, West Indies, Thailand, Nigeria, South Korea, Singapore, Qatar, Sri Lanka....aaaand right until NYE in the Maldives!

Loved sharing a range of new music be it the record breaking 9 m...inute qawaali rendition of Tajdare Haram for Coke Studio, a close to my heart collaboration with my favourite folk singer Mai Dhai, multiple singles for Tseries ‪#‎Arahahumein‬, over half a dozen film soundtrack singles for big banner Indian and upcoming brilliant Pakistani cinema .... A nationwide UAE campaign for Etisalat.. Luvved every moment of each toil....It's a nice feeling now to begin 2016 with a nomination at the prestigious Flimfare awards in India for best Bollywood playback singer for my song ‪#‎JeenaJeena‬ from the wonderful movie ‪#‎Badlapur‬ (which is also nominated for Best film of the year!) Congratulations to all the excellent talent out there.

A special thanks to all my special aadeez & well wishers for sharing the ride with me. Wishing you all a productive & progressive 2016. smile emoticon

Luv n light,
Atif
